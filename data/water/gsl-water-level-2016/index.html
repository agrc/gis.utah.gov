Utah Great Salt Lake Water Level 2016

This dataset was derived from the [breakline features](https://storage.googleapis.com/state-of-utah-sgid-downloads/lidar/great-salt-lake-2016/GSL_shps.zip) associated with the [2016 Great Salt Lake LiDAR project]({% link data/elevation-and-terrain/2016-lidar-gsl/index.md %}). To create this dataset the _lake Pond_ features were extracted from the _GreatSaltLake_Breaklines_ shapefile and converted into polygons. All polygons outside of the main north and south arms of the lake were removed. The _Elevation_m_ & _Elevation_ft_ values represent the elevation of the water when the LiDAR was acquired.
