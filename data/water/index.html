Water Related Land Use map data UGRC Water related land use in Utah. Other Water Data

  • A variety of other water-themed datasets exist depicting springs, streams, and lakes from USGS 100K and 24K digital line graphs ( DLGs). Contact UGRC for more information. These include polygon representations of larger stream channels.
  • Stream Gauges NHD
  • National Hydrography Dataset (NHD)
  • Great Salt Lake Meander Line (Cadastral)
