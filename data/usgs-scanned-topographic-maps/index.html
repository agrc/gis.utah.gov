Vintage

The Utah Geological Survey created digital 15- and 30-minute vintage U.S. Geological Survey topographic quadrangle maps (1900-1966) by scanning original paper maps at a resolution of 500 dots per inch (dpi). These digital scans were enhanced in Adobe Photoshop as needed. Subsequent 16 point spline georeferencing was done in ArcMap. The resulting TIFF files are served on the AGRC ImageServer with on-the-fly collar clipping, producing a seamless map where adjacent quadrangle maps are available.
