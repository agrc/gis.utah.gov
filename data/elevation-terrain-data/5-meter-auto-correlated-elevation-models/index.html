Quality Assurance: Report

AGRC provides a statewide coverage of 5 Meter Auto-Correlated DEMs. The DEMs wereÂ created from the imagery collected during the 2006 NAIP aerial photography flight. The auto-correlation process is not as rigorous as other methods of elevation modeling such as photogrammetry, lidar mapping, radar mapping, etc, and therefore end-users should be aware that anomalies are expected within the elevation dataset. In comparison to the USGS NED dataset, the 5-meter DEM provides higher resolution and horizontal accuracy but anomalies are present within the data.

The DEM files are stored as .asc formatted files and are ~125 mb per 20,000 X 20,000 meter block. This data is in a UTM NAD83 meters projection and has a Vertical Accuracy of RMSE 4m - NSSDA 95% of 9.8m and a Horizontol Accuracy of RMSE 3m, NSSDA 95% of 5.2m. Please reference this Auto_Correlated_DEM_5m shapefile as an index to determine which files to download.
